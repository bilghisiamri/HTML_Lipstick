Profile: Lipstick Hanasui

Hanasui is a popular lipstick brand known for its high-quality and stylish products. The brand offers a wide range of shades and finishes, from classic reds to bold pinks and nudes.

History

Hanasui was founded in 2010 by makeup artist and entrepreneur, Sarah Lee. With a passion for creating high-quality and affordable lipsticks, Sarah set out to create a brand that would cater to women of all ages and backgrounds.

Mission

At Hanasui, our mission is to empower women to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin. We believe that every woman deserves to feel gorgeous, and our lipsticks are designed to help them do just that.

Values

We are committed to creating high-quality, cruelty-free, and affordable lipsticks. We believe in being kind to the planet and all its inhabitants, and we strive to minimize our environmental impact in everything we do.
